# Home
> Present the catalog of all feature demos and own the site shell — navigation and design tokens.

## Boundary
- `browse-catalog` — view the index of every feature demo, grouped by business component

## Requirements
### R1: Catalog
- R1.1 — The BC shall list every feature page of every business component, grouped by business component.
- R1.2 — The BC shall link each catalog entry to its feature page.
- R1.3 — The BC shall show for each entry the feature name and the JavaScript it replaces.
- R1.4 — The BC shall show each entry's Baseline status.

### R2: Site shell
- R2.1 — The BC shall provide the design tokens and shared stylesheet consumed by every page.

## Out of scope
- The feature demos themselves — owned by `overlays`, `forms`, `motion`, and `structure`.
- Search, filtering, personalization.
