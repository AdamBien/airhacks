:user-valid / :user-invalid

Baseline: Widely available · since 2026-05

Replaces: validation libraries and the touched/dirty state tracking they exist for. Constraints live in the markup; the :user-* pseudo-classes wait for interaction instead of flagging pristine fields.

Fields stay neutral until you interact. Commit an invalid value (Tab away) to see feedback; submitting with an empty required field is blocked by the browser.
